***
The use of {create_index.py} file 
****
{create_index.py} is like building the map of your product space before you start driving around.
Without it, your search engine would have to “re-draw the map” every single time a user searches.

[create_index.py] (offline) → builds index.faiss + id_map.npy
     ⬇
[main.py API] (online) → loads saved index into memory
     ⬇
Search queries use the prebuilt index to find top matches in milliseconds
